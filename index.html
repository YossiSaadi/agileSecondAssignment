Agile - 2nd assignment - Football API

List of APIs

  • GET /team/:teamId gives you a list of matches of a team by its id
  • GET /team/:teamId/:status gives you a list of matches of a team by its id and status
  • GET /tournament/:tournamentId gives you a list of matches of a tournament by its id
  • GET /tournament/:tournamentId/:status gives you a list of matches of a tournament by its id and status
